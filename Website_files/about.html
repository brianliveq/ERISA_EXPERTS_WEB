Our Mission

Eliminate fiduciary risk for plan sponsors — completely.

Most ERISA service providers offer guidance, recommendations, and quarterly reports. ERISA Experts does something different: we formally accept fiduciary responsibility. That means we are legally accountable for the decisions made on your plan — not just the advice we give.

Our clients are HR leaders, CFOs, and business owners who are tired of carrying exposure they don't fully understand and can't easily shed. We built our firm to solve that exact problem.

What We Stand For

Principles that guide every engagement.

01

Accountability First

We accept fiduciary status in writing on every engagement. There is no ambiguity about who is responsible for which task — and no way to walk it back.

02

Clarity Over Complexity

ERISA is complex. We translate it. Our clients always know exactly where they stand, what's been done, and what's next — in plain language.

03

Action, Not Reports

We don't send a PDF and wait for the next call. We act continuously on behalf of our clients — documenting every step in a way that satisfies regulatory scrutiny.

Why We Exist

Built by people who saw the gap firsthand.

Our founders spent years inside the ERISA advisory world and kept seeing the same pattern: plan sponsors were told they were protected, only to discover — often during litigation — that their advisors bore no legal liability whatsoever.

ERISA Experts was created to close that gap. We are an independent fiduciary firm — not affiliated with any broker-dealer, record keeper, or investment company — which means our only obligation is to your plan and its participants.

Independence Matters
